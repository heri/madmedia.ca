About

Heri is an engineer with an Msc. from Telecom SudParis, a french engineering school and a post-graduate degree from HEC Montréal, Canada.

Heri designs and builds systems in Ruby, JS, Go, Erlang for web and mobile.

He is interested in projects that are difficult, worth solving and with good people.

Heri has also written about technology, innovation, and design.

He works with startups, engineers, entrepreneurs, and speaks about technology in meetups and conferences.

In his free time, he is in design and visual arts, with 2 photography expositions, one short film, and many visual works showcased through Howl! Arts in Montréal.
